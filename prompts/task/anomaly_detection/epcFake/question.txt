## knowns
- EPC format: 6 parts separated by dots (header.company.product.lot.manufacture.serial)
- Valid header: "001"
- Valid company codes: {"8804823", "8805843", "8809437"}
- Product code: 7 digits
- Lot code: 6 digits  
- Serial: 9 digits
- Manufacture date: YYYYMMDD format, not future, not older than 5 years
